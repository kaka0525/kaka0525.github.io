About

I am a product designer and software engineer that lives in Seattle, WA. I am an empathetic researcher that strive to design and create clean and intuitive products.

My training in Political Science hones my skills in problem solving, critical thinking, and research and it instills in me with compassion and understanding to people. My experience working as a software developer gave me a broader perspective of the product development process. I consider myself a UX generalist that can pick up new skills and adapt well to new concepts and technologies. I believe there are always opportunities for improvement and eager to learn new things and broaden my horizon.
